LIZARRAN

  • GREENSPRINGS

In Lizaran, you can enjoy seafood from fishing ports nationwide, fresh vegetables and meat from producers in the Tama area, mainly in Tachikawa, and Pinchos, a specialty of Rezaran. It is natural wine mainly from Spain, craft beer produced in Tachikawa, and colorful Moku Teru. Relax in an atmosphere suitable for special occasions such as anniversaries and birthdays. In addition to the meals served in the store as a take-out menu, we also offer a “Comprehensive Nutrition Food” supervised by veterinarian for pets.

  • Place: E3/1F,GREEN SPRINGS,3-1,Midoricho,Tachikawa-shi,Tokyo
  • Operating hours: 11:00-22:00(LO21:00)
  • TEL: 042-512-5198
  • URL: http://lizarranjp.jp/
