      Restaurant

      Sound, aroma, and motion. Recharge yourself in the morning with the vigorous spirit of the kitchen.

      Our lively and active kitchen will elevate your expectations for breakfast. Listen to the sound of crackling firewood and watch the flames as our chefs prepare our homemade grilled ham, bake fragrant breads, and cook Eggs Benedict just for you.

      Our Original Eggs Benedict

      The bun is toasted until aromatic, then topped with a generous portion of poached eggs, bacon, and our special hollandaise or cheese fondue sauce.

      Indulge in a luxurious morning with our freshly baked and
      handcrafted natural yeast bread.

      Homemade bread

      Both our hotel bread and baguettes can be sliced by guests for personal consumption.

      Homemade ham, sausage, and bacon

      We have a selection of carefully selected flavors, including the domestic bologna ham, "Bierschinken", herb-scented sausage, and "roast bimei pork" from Ibaraki Prefecture.

      Let your body absorb the essence of our natural ingredients.

      Our Salads & Original Dressings

      Enjoy with your favorite seasonings, including several types of dressing (French, green shiso (perilla), southern dressing), olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and white wine vinegar.

      Donuts

      We have an assortment of cute and colorful mini donuts. Please enjoy as many as you like from our section.

      Pancakes

      Enjoy freshly made pancakes from our pancake machine, created right before your eyes. Add fruit jam and honey to make your pancakes even more appetizing!

      We offer a selection of Japanese dishes in addition to a wide variety of Western dishes.

      Feel the morning breeze and sunshine on our garden terrace.

      Our relaxing seats on the terrace in the garden will have you soaking up the sun among the trees and grass. Wake up in the morning to find yourself in this lovely forest resort and start your day refreshed with our breakfast.

      We offer truly delicious meals with our own flair.

      Our master chef: TAKAMI, Hiroshi

      The mainstay of our breakfast is homemade bread baked in-store using natural yeast and salt. Coupled with the theme of the breakfast buffet, you can find many side dishes that suit the taste of the fresh bread. When you think that you want to eat something really delicious in the morning, you'll find out local-made ham, cheese, and egg dishes. Please enjoy our many homemade dishes.
