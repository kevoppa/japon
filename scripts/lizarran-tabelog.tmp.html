Transportation

Approximately an 8-minute walk from JR Chuo Line Tachikawa Station and about a 4-minute walk from Tama Toshi Monorail Tachikawa Kita Station.

Upon exiting the pedestrian deck at the north exit of JR Tachikawa Station, head towards Tachikawa Kita Station of the monorail while keeping Isetan on your right. Proceed along the pathway under the monorail. After descending to Sansan Road, continue straight, and you'll find Green Springs on your left, located at the back of the first floor.

Food All-You-Can-Drink Wine! Our Colorful and Proud Lunch Buffet

At Rizaran Tachikawa, our lunch features a vibrant buffet with over 20 dishes, complemented by unlimited wine for a truly indulgent experience. Enjoy our new tacos, salads made with fresh Tachikawa vegetables, and a variety of desserts. Plus, you can choose your main dish, served fresh right at your table. This is our signature lunch, popular on both weekdays and weekends.

Food Vegetables from Koyama Farm and Tender Pork from Shimoda Livestock

We use colorful, fresh vegetables from Koyama Farm in Tachikawa and the tender, flavorful "Yawa Pork" from Shimoda Livestock. The vegetables shine in salads and tapas, while the Yawa Pork brings out rich flavors in grilled and simmered dishes. We focus on local ingredients and deliver dishes that highlight seasonal blessings. Enjoy the unique local charm that you can only experience here.

SpaceAll Tapas for JPY 290 and Wine! A Casual Bar Atmosphere

The stand bar area is perfect for a casual stop after work or while shopping. The showcase features over 10 daily tapas, all priced at just JPY 290. You can also enjoy a glass of wine starting from JPY 290, making it great for anything from a quick drink to a gathering with friends. Experience a lively atmosphere and enjoy a taste of Spain in a relaxed setting.

SpaceDining with Paella and Authentic Cuisine in Private Rooms

In our dining area, you can leisurely enjoy authentic Spanish cuisine, including our signature paella, seafood, and meat dishes. We have two private rooms that are perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, business meetings, or family gatherings, creating a special atmosphere for your important moments. Unlike the open and lively bar area, these private spaces offer a calm environment where you can savor your meal and wine at a relaxed pace. Whether it's an everyday meal or a special occasion, we cater to a wide range of dining experiences.
